# Formulary sheet 

## function spaces  

### normed vectorspace  

$\forall f \in V$ the following should hold such that $||\cdot||$ is a norm on $V$:

$||f|| < \infty$   
$||f|| \ge 0$  
$f \equiv 0 \Longleftrightarrow ||f|| = 0$  
$\forall a \in \R : ||af|| = |a| \cdot ||f||$  
$\forall g \in V : ||f+g|| \le ||f|| + ||g||$
